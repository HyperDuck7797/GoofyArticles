What is The Colora-Dole Conspiracy?

The Colora-Dole Conspiracy is a conspiracy theory made by Flaming Hapless Nachos in 2022. On the Flaming Hapless Nachos YouTube channel, you can find his video about the topic but, as their channel is rather small, it has not gained that much traction among conspiracy theorists. The basic substance of the theory is that Dole, the fruit company, is actually a shadow government, that has been slowly taking over the world. As the name suggests, this theory claims that the center of all of Dole's world control is being done in the state of Colorado. In they video Mr. Nachos goes on to make comparisons between the Dole logo and the state flag of Colorado, citing it as a source of evidence for their claim.
